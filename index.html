About Us
We're the Shiva Atomatrons, a robotics club from Shiva Robotics Academy in Jacksonville, Florida. Our team has won awards and gone to the State Championship Tournament at the middle school level, called FLL. But this is our first year in FTC, the high school level of competition. So we have lots to learn, and lots of new challenges to meet. Learn more about our team!
Atomobot
Our team is building our robot, fondly named Atomobot, from scratch. Designing, building and programming our robot is an interative Agile process, where we constantly redesign, rebuild and reprogram our robot to solve problems and make it better. We will use our robot to play this year's competition game, Ultimate Goal!

Our team participates in the FIRST Tech Challenge, a robotics competition for teams of up to 15 students from 7th to 12th grade.

Each year, FIRST designs a game. This year's game is called Ultimate Challenge! Teams from around the world compete to build the best robot for the game, using a wide variety of metal, plastic and electronics parts.

FTC is a game that challenges kids to learn about physical systems, electronics, and programming, as well as budgeting and being part of a team.
